## Viewing Report

screenshot

### Red Flags
- High traffic with low activation: unclear onboarding
- High tutorial starts with low completions: too long or missing steps
- High GitHub clicks with low engagement: weak README or unclear next steps

### Green Flags
- 7-day return visits above 20%
- Tutorial completion above 40%
- Docs-to-signup above 3%

Weekly DevRel Workflow

### 10-Minute Monday Check
1. Top five content pages
2. Conversion rates
3. Drop-off points

### Decision Rules
- High traffic with under 2 percent activation: rewrite
- Over 5 percent activation: repurpose into a video, tutorial, or talk

### Retire or Rewrite
Content under 1 percent activation after 30 days should be archived or reworked.

Use this sentence:

"This content drove X percent more activations and reduced time-to-first-success by Y percent."
